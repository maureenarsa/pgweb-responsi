Geographical Conditions of Sleman Regency

"Sleman has geographical conditions that have great potential to be used as gardens because it is located on the slopes of a volcano with fertile volcanic soil. Apart from that, the availability of abundant water is also an important factor in supporting gardens in the area. By utilizing its natural potential, Sleman has a great opportunity to become a center for plantations and the development of various crops with high economic potential.
